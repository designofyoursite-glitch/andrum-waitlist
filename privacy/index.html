Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2025
01
Introduction

Welcome to Andrum ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit andrum.space and join our waitlist.

By submitting your email address on our website, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

02
Information We Collect

We collect only the following personal information:

  • Email address — provided voluntarily when you join our waitlist.

We do not collect names, payment information, location data, or any other personal data at this stage.

03
How We Use Your Information

We use your email address solely to:

  • Notify you when Andrum launches and becomes available.
  • Send you early access to our guided breathwork practices, as described on our website.
  • Communicate updates about the product, if you have consented to receive them.

We will not use your email for marketing unrelated to Andrum, and we will not sell or rent your information to third parties.

04
How We Store Your Data

Your email address is currently stored in Google Sheets, a service provided by Google LLC. Google's servers may be located in the United States and other countries. By submitting your email, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States.

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

05
Data Retention

We will retain your email address until one of the following occurs:

  • You request removal from our waitlist.
  • The Andrum product launches and you either create an account or are removed from the waitlist.
  • We determine the project will not launch.
06
Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Deletion — request that we delete your email address from our records at any time.
  • Withdrawal of consent — unsubscribe or opt out of communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at: andrum.space@gmail.com

07
Users in the European Union (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

Our legal basis for processing your email address is your explicit consent, given when you submitted the waitlist form.

08
California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to request deletion of your personal information, and the right not to be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights.

To make a request, contact us at: andrum.space@gmail.com

09
Third-Party Services

We currently use the following third-party service to store waitlist data:

  • Google Sheets (Google LLC) — for storing submitted email addresses. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy

We do not use advertising trackers, analytics cookies, or any other third-party tracking tools on our website at this time.

10
Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted their email to our waitlist, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

11
Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

12
Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

Website: andrum.space
